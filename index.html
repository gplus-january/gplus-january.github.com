What is G+ January?

G+ January is a celebration of Google+ and all things good that come along with using it. It came about when two ordinary guys, +Daniel Pritchett and +Ravi Nath, were discussing the pros and cons of Google+ when compared to other social networks. They soon discovered the con was the lack of people they knew that actually used Google+. They came up with the idea of G+ January to help promote the use of Google+ and its many wonderful features, and hopefully win some of their friends over and transform them into "Plussers."

How it works

Well first thing's first, you need to create a google account, if you don't already have one, and sign in to Google+.

Once you are officially a "Plusser" you can help out the cause by:

  • Making public posts using the #gplusjanuary hashtag.
  • Inviting friends to join Google+ and explore its many features.


Wait... what's in it for me?

At this point you may be wondering what's the point to all of this. Trust us your effort will not go to waste. Google+ has many great features that you may like, you definitely owe it to yourself to take a look at them. Soon you will see how awesome Google+ is, and it only gets better with more of your friends involved. That's what G+ January is all about, getting people you know involved in Google+.

Google+ Features

Google+ is a feature-rich social network making it increasingly impossible to detail all of the great features it offers, but we'll give it a try. For even more info you can check out the official Learn More page from Google or take a tour from inside Google+ itself by clicking on the button from the home page and selecting "Take a tour."

Home Page

The gateway to everything Google+ has to offer. From here you can make posts, search for posts, read posts from friends in all of your circles, check whats trending, make new friends.

Trending Topics
Trending topics highlight the trends of public posts that Plussers are making based on both hashtags and the contents of what is being posted. This is a great way to catch up on the latest minute-to-minute buzz on the web.

Trending topics appear to the side of the main stream on the home page and appear on several other pages as well. Be sure to check them out.

Profile

Your profile is your identity on Google+ it is where other Plussers can go to look you up. You can choose to share as little or as much information as you wish with the world. You can view posts that you've made as well as make entirely new posts. You can also view any photos and videos you've shared and uploaded.

Profile and Cover photos
It's your space... decorate it like you want it. Your profile picture is similar to those used by other social networks. Your profile picture appears next to your posts and comments and allows others to identify you quickly without the need for names. So say cheese and say hello to Google+!

Your cover picture is a decorative image used to keep your profile looking sharp and reflecting who you are.

Circles

Circles are similar to lists in that they organize your friends. They also allow you to filter your home page down to posts by friends within a specific circle. They allow you to separate different groups that you belong to easily which then allows you to make posts that target just the people you want to. Unlike some other social sites you don't have to "friend" someone to add them to a circle or "circle them."

Circle on the fly

When Plussing in the wild you may come across cool new people you would like to add to one or more of your many circles. Perhaps you are even so inspired you want to create a brand-spanking new circle. Doing so is not a problem with this neat feature, simply mouse over their profile picture or name and mini-profile for them pops up that allows you to place them in one of your circles or add them to a new circle.

Communities

Communities allow you to participate in discussions about topics you enjoy with people who also enjoy them, regardless if they are in your circles or not.
